The Real Problem

The ban wasn't the failure.
The missing infrastructure was.

Responsible dog owners follow rules when they have the tools to do so. Most don't skip cleanup out of malice they skip it because there was no station, no bag, no obvious place to go.

The old approach

  • Assume owners bring their own bags most forget
  • No designated relief areas dogs go wherever
  • No signage reinforcing expectations
  • No consequences visible, no social nudge
  • Mall staff deal with cleanup reactively
  • Ban the dogs when it gets bad enough

The new approach

  • ATD × Earth Rated stations at every entrance bags, wipes & pads always available
  • Designated relief zones with clear signage and astroturf
  • Behavioral nudge: "Most dog owners here clean up. Be one of them."
  • The Royalmount Dog Code a community standard, not a rule
  • ATD location as certified welcome anchor
  • Dogs welcome again. Problem solved at the source.

"People need concrete steps and protocols to follow. It's still not second nature and that's not their fault."

The Royalmount Dog Code

Simple rules.
Real results.

Behavior changes when expectations are clear, tools are available, and the community reinforces the norm. Here's how it works.

1
📋

Acknowledge the Code

Scan QR or receive card at entrance. Takes 10 seconds. Sets the expectation.

2
🧻

Grab Your Bags

Free bags & wipes at every ATD × Earth Rated station. No excuse to be unprepared.

3
🐕

Use the Relief Zone

Designated outdoor areas before entering. The right behavior made easy.

4
✅

Clean Up. Always.

Bins everywhere. Social signage makes it a community norm, not just a rule.

5
🌟

Be an Ambassador

Owners who use the program become the program. Community owns the standard.

The behavioral science: Most people don't violate norms out of malice the friction of doing the right thing is too high. Lower the friction (free bags, visible bins, clear zones), add social reinforcement, and compliance approaches near-100%. This is the same model that transformed public smoking behavior.
